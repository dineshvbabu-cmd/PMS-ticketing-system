Status Guide — How tickets move through the lifecycle ▾
📬
Open
A vessel or user has sent an email to PMS Support. No engineer has replied yet. The issue is waiting to be picked up.
🔧
In Progress
An engineer has replied and is actively working on it. The thread is ongoing and a resolution has not yet been confirmed.
✅
Resolved
Engineer confirmed the task is done — e.g. "vessel is able to proceed / requisition raised / issue has been resolved". Also set when the engineer sent the original email (outgoing reply).
🔒
Closed
The vessel or user acknowledged the resolution — e.g. "Thank you / Noted / Confirmed". The ticket is fully complete with both sides confirming.
Auto-detection rules: Status is auto-assigned from email content and reply count. You can always override it manually by clicking the status buttons on any ticket. Tickets older than 30 days with replies are automatically marked Resolved — reflecting the team's day-to-day resolution practice. Tickets with 3+ replies older than 7 days are also auto-resolved.
📋 Report category: Emails detected as automated/scheduled reports (weekly overdue, sync reports, daily/monthly PMS reports) are always auto-classified as Report and immediately marked Resolved — they are informational and require no action.
